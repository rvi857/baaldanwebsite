We are Texas Nach Baliye

The University of Texas’s Premier Co-ed Bollywood Fusion Team

Founded in 2008 by a group of friends, our team has evolved into a nationally competitive dance team that brings a new spin to Bollywood dance. Our team mixes Bollywood, hip-hop, classical, bhangra and other styles to create routines that are innovative and entertaining.

Texas Nach Baliye competes nationally in what is known as the Bollywood America circuit, against the best Bollywood fusion dance teams in the country. You can also find us competing locally in Texas, both on campus and elsewhere in the state. We also can be hired to perform for weddings and other events.
